Investor Information

We understand the importance of protecting your investments in the gold trade.

Investor & Lender Risk

The application developed by Aurum79 significantly mitigates the risk for investors and lenders by providing a comprehensive tracking system for the physical gold throughout its journey from the source to the refinery. This ensures transparency and accountability at every step, reducing the potential risks associated with the investment or lending.

Price Volatility

To manage price volatility, AURUM79 adopts a strategic approach. We primarily pre-sell the gold to the buyer, offering a slight fixed discount off the LBMA rates. This agreement is based on a minimum one-year contract supply. By securing these pre-sale agreements in advance, we can negotiate the price and purchase terms with local aggregators, thereby minimizing price fluctuations.

Cash Management

To ensure effective cash management, the buyer is required to provide a bank guarantee in the form of a Letter of Credit (L/C), Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC), or Escrow. This bank guarantee is uploaded onto our platform, and the settlement is facilitated via SWIFT based on the final assay report, which is verified on the ledgers.
